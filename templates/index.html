    This UI triggers your custom 4-step Python pipeline.

    1. Save File(s): Uploaded Excel files are saved to data/excel_folder.
    2. Step 1 (Convert): The pipeline scans for new .xlsx files and converts them to .csv in data/csv_folder.
    3. Step 2 (Clean): New .csv files are cleaned, formatted, and saved in-place.
    4. Step 3 (Append): Cleaned .csv data is appended to data/processed/master_dataset.csv.
    5. Step 4 (Reshape): The master file is reshaped and de-duplicated into the final data/processed/master_dataset_cleaned.csv.
    6. Step 5 (Train): The cleaned data is used to train a new Random Forest model, which is saved to logs/random_forest_model.pkl.
